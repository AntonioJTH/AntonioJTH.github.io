About Me

I am originally from Querétaro, Mexico. I studied my bachelor's degree at UAQ, my master's degree at UASLP and my Ph.D. at UNAM. Currently, I have a postdoctoral position in the Department of Mathematics at the University of California, Davis.

I really enjoy math, reading, hiking, swimming, and going out into nature with my dogs.
